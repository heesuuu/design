[2018 Architectural Graphics of 12 Architects]

I worked on a project called "Architectural Graphics of 12 Architects," focusing on the theme of 'architecture.' I selected 12 contemporary architects including Tadao Ando, Philip Johnson, Daniel Libeskind, Zaha Hadid, Norman Foster, and Rem Koolhaas, and graphically expressed the spirit they sought through architecture.
Since the spirit of buildings cannot be simply represented in 2D graphics, I developed the project to allow users to transform it into three dimensions using various paper properties such as assembly, disassembly, and folding through paper cutting.

In addition to graphic design, I was responsible for the overall structure of the calendar package and the production of desk calendars.

November : RATIONAL FRAME | Philip Johnson
December : PILGRIM`S LIGHT | Ando Tadao
