BeV Card Package Design
Client | KB KookminCard
2016. 05 - 2018. 08
Role

Planning / Research (40%)
Package (40%)
Guide Book (90%)
Brochure (80%)
Coupon (40%)

I have been involved in the overall package design work for the BeV card series, a premium card series launched by KB Kookmin Card. After introducing the BeV V card initially, we sequentially released BeV III and BeV IX. In addition to package design, I designed comprehensive guides, coupon guides, vouchers, etc., so that customers could consistently experience KB Kookmin Card's premium services at all touchpoints from applying for the card to using it.
