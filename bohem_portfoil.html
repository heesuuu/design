BOHEM Skinny Package Design
Client | KT&G
2018. 03 - 2018. 07
Role

Project Manager
Planning / Research (60%)
Graphic Illust (80%)

This project involves creating the fourth series of BOHEM CIGAR WRAPPER. Since tobacco products have limited media and frequency for advertising, package design serves as the only channel of communication with consumers, acting as a link between the brand and consumers. Maintaining the unique circular symbol of the Bohem product line, the goal is to emphasize product characteristics and express an exotic sensibility by applying bright colors and designs inspired by tropical plants. The fourth series was launched in December 2018 and is currently being sold in convenience stores nationwide.
