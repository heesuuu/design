Brunt is a brand that started in 2016 based in Seoul, asking whether it is possible to create better products by combining cutting-edge technology and user experience design.
An item commonly used in daily life, when interpreted by Brunt, creates a new experience with usability and design redefined. In 2019, Brunt introduced Brunt Company, an expanded concept from the existing brand, and launched the official Brunt online store, selling a variety of lifestyle products. The common customer value pursued by Brunt Company's products and services is to help customers find a better lifestyle and a better quality of life through their products and services.
