Kulry Christmas Edition 2022
Lalasweet
2022
Role

Package Structure (90%)
Graphic Design (60%)

In celebration of Christmas 2022, I designed a Christmas edition package exclusively for the distribution channel brand, 'Kurly Market'. To provide customers with a special experience, I created the package in a pop-up structure. When you lift the top holder of the Christmas package, gold foil graphics appear.
The package was sold temporarily at 'Kurly Market' during the month of December and was completely sold out.
