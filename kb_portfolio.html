KB Kookmin Card BX
Client | KB KookminCard
2016. 05 - 2018. 08
Role

Planning / Research (30%)
Card Design (40%)
Online Promotion Page Design (20%)
Marketing Tools Design (40%)

Since 2016, I have been working on various offline productions for KB Kookmin Card, including card design and formats, while maintaining the brand identity. I introduced a new icon design by borrowing motifs from the Kookmin Card font and expanded this to create Kookmin Card's own illustration style. Additionally, by utilizing KB Kookmin Card's colors and fonts, I created new pictograms to maintain consistency in identity and build practical design from the customer's perspective. Through a more concise and clear concept centered around the customer, I actively utilized brand-specific marketing tools.
