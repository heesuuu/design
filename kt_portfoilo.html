LKT Biz Character Design
Client | KT
2016. 05 - 2016. 10
Role

Project Manager
Character Design (100%)
Emoticon (100%)

I worked on creating characters and emoticons for the chat service within the KT Enterprise Phone service, which is a service launched by KT that allows companies to easily and quickly manage essential business tasks on mobile and PC.
To develop characters that users, who are company employees, can relate to, I conducted planning and research before completing the final work, including up to 50 moving emoticons.
