LG Weather APP Illust
Client | LG
2016. 07
Role

Illust (20%)

I worked on the illustrations for the weather app inside the LG V30.
Each illustration depicts a natural landscape corresponding to the weather concept, portrayed in a flat style in line with the 2016 trend.
To ensure visibility of the weather information, I toned down the illustrations overall and arranged them in the optimal layout to make the main information stand out most effectively.
