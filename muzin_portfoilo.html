MUZIN Web Magazine Design
Client | National Museum of Korea
2016. 06 - 2018. 05
Role

Editorial (20%)
Webtoon (100%)

MUZIN is a webzine presented by the National Museum of Korea. It aims to provide information to users on various topics ranging from artifacts in the National Museum of Korea to donors and exhibitions.
I worked on columns for issues 73 and 74, ensuring usability for both web and mobile platforms. Additionally, starting in June 2016, the "Donation, Widely Spread" column has been serialized in a webtoon format, which was reprocessed and uploaded.

Designed & Developed by Heesu Lee. 2024
