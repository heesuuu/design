SBS Channel Branding 2018
Client | SBS
2017. 04 - 2017. 12
Role

Planning / Research (80%)
Motif Source (70%)
Graphic (50%)
Design Guideline (100%)

SBS, the youngest terrestrial broadcaster launched in 1990, is a private industry broadcaster that broadcasts TV and radio. While the existing SBS used a graphic motif derived from 'Media Tree' and 'Eyes of Wisdom', it was lacking in delivering a consistent tone, image, and message compared to others in the industry. To improve this, a transformation was aimed at SBS's own creative and youthful channel image, requiring the integrated image construction of a channel that can effectively communicate the values of the SBS Media Group.
